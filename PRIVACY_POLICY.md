# Privacy Policy for SmartDash by Shawn Rain

Last updated: 2026-04-07

`SmartDash by Shawn Rain` ("SmartDash", "we", "our", or "the app") is an Android application for vehicle dashboard telemetry, ride logging, controller connectivity, and optional cloud backup.

This Privacy Policy explains what data the app may access, how that data is used, and what choices you have.

## 1. Data We Access

SmartDash may access the following categories of data, depending on which features you use:

- Bluetooth data, including nearby BLE device information and controller/BMS telemetry needed for scanning, connecting, reading live data, and writing supported settings
- Location data, when required by Android for BLE scanning on some Android versions and when you use GPS-based speed, ride recording, or wheel calibration features
- Ride data, including speed, power, voltage, current, distance, temperature, trip history, and user-created vehicle profiles stored locally on your device
- Google account basic identity data, such as your account email, when you choose to sign in for Google Drive backup and restore
- App diagnostic logs that you explicitly export or share

## 2. How We Use Data

We use data only to provide app features, including:

- connecting to compatible BLE devices
- displaying dashboard telemetry and ride statistics
- saving settings, vehicle profiles, and trip history on-device
- calibrating speed and wheel circumference
- backing up and restoring your data through Google Drive
- helping you troubleshoot issues when you choose to export logs

We do not sell your personal data.

## 3. Google Drive Backup and OAuth

If you choose to use Google Drive sync or backup:

- the app requests Google authorization from your Google account
- the app uses the Google Drive `drive.appdata` scope, which is limited to the app's private application data folder
- backup data is encrypted before upload
- the app does not use this permission to read or manage your general Google Drive files outside the app-specific storage area

Google account access is optional. If you do not sign in, the app continues to work without Google Drive backup features.

## 4. Data Storage

Most app data is stored locally on your device.

If you enable Google Drive backup, encrypted backup files are stored in your Google Drive app data area. Backup encryption is designed to support restoring data across your devices that use the same Google account.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties except in these limited cases:

- with Google, only when you explicitly use Google Sign-In and Google Drive backup features
- with apps or services you choose when you export logs, share ride files, share posters, or export CSV data

## 6. Permissions

SmartDash may request permissions such as Bluetooth, nearby devices, notifications, and location. These permissions are used only for core app functionality related to telemetry, connectivity, ride measurement, update download, and backup features.

## 7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect data handled by the app. However, no method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be completely secure.

## 8. Your Choices

You can:

- use the app without Google Drive backup
- sign out of Google within the app
- delete local app data from your device
- remove the app's data from your Google account according to Google's account and Drive controls
- choose whether to export or share logs and ride files

## 9. Children's Privacy

SmartDash is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be published in this repository at this file path.
